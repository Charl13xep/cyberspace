At Cyberspace our goal is to help make the internet safer. So far, we've raised awareness by exposing methods used by real blackhat hackers and showing how to secure systems from these hackers. CyberVPN allows us to take another step towards a safer internet that is free from censorship and surveillance. CyberVPN allows users to protect their privacy using our state of the art servers that use unbreakable AES encryption.
